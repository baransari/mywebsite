Analysis & Conclusion

Based on the survey, the majority of users purchase online at least once a month, particularly those between the ages of 18–39. Amazon and Trendyol are the most preferred platforms, indicating a high level of trust and variety. Users with a college degree or higher are more likely to shop online frequently.

Supporting Statistics

According to Pew Internet, nearly 80% of U.S. adults say they shop online.
eMarketer reports that e-commerce sales globally reached over $6 trillion in 2024, driven largely by mobile purchases.
ClickZ highlights that younger consumers expect fast delivery and user-friendly mobile apps.
E-Commerce Times confirms that consumer trust in online platforms continues to grow annually.
